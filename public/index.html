What Is DAO

A DAO is a member-owned community and is a safe method to collaborate with internet strangers in a joint project or initiative enabling like-minded folks to connect around the globe. It is an organization or company that is collectively owned and managed by its members through an online community.
DAOs have built-in treasuries that no one has the authority to access without the approval of the group. Decisions are governed by proposals and voting to ensure everyone in the organisation has a voice This prevents anyone in the company from authorising spending based on their own whims. Everything is out in the open and the rules around spending company money are coded into the DAO via smart contracts.
All major company decisions are made by governance token holders creating a strong sense of community and ownership of the project.

About Crypto-528

Crypto 528 is a unique Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) which is completely owned and operated by it’s token holders. Crypto 528 is a community owned DAO that is fundraising to open and operate a cryptocurrency mine in the country of Georgia. The company will mine six crypto currencies which include Bitcoin, Ethereum, Zcash, Litecoin, Nervos and Decred.
Each month after the operating costs of the mine are paid, the remaining mined crypto will be used to:
  1. Donate 10% to charities chosen by the DAO. Initially this will be given to 5 charities which are:
    • Red Cross
    • Save the Children
    • Greenpeace
    • SOS Children’s Villages (Tbilisi)
    • Autism Speaks
  2. Keep 10% of the mined crypto in a company wallet to be used for expansion or contingency
  3. Distribute the remaining 80% of all mined crypto for the month as dividends to token holders

Based on our conservative financial model, the dividend yield will be 65% in the first year of operation.

The DAO will issue two types of tokens:
  1. C-MINE which is a non-governance token that entitles holders to the monthly crypto dividends paid out by the mine. C-MINE tokens are stakeable allowing holders to earn governance tokens.
  2. Governance tokens (C-528) enable holders the right to vote in the DAO on all organization decisions and policies. C-528 tokens can also be traded on decentralized exchanges.

Our goal is to create an organization that is completed controlled by an online community of token holders.
