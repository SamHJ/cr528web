The location of the Crypto 528 mine is in Georgia, located in a 300sqm (3,200sqft) warehouse in the Tbilisi Free Trade Zone located 20km outside of Tbilisi the country’s capital city.
The Tbilisi Free Zone JSC was launched in November 2015 on 170,000 m2 of land.
The BitFury Group, one of the world’s leading blockchain technology companies is the owner and developer of the property.
They also own and operate a facility in the Tbilisi Free Trade Zone as seen below. Tbilisi Free Trade Zone Map The benefits of operating in the Tbilisi Free Trade Zone include:
  1. Electrical costs of $0.04 kw/h
  2. No tax on profits earned
  3. No tax on dividends paid
  4. No property tax
  5. No customs duties paid on importing equipment
  6. Crypto 528 Unit in the Tbilisi Free Trade Zone (Exterior)
  7. Crypto 528 Unit in the Tbilisi Free Trade Zone (Interior)
